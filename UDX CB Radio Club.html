About UDX CB Radio Club

UDX was originally formed during the 1970s in the state of Utah, back then it was called Utah Delta X-Ray, most club members residing within the state of Utah.

In order to join the club one had "to know somebody". Back in the mid 70s I was a teenager, when I asked to join I was turned down. (I asked more than once :) Being the Club did not have a huge roster and the majority of the members lived in Utah the Club fizzled out at the end of the great CB boom.

I moved from SLC to Washington state in 2002, during the early 2000s I started using the number UDX-390 ( 390 has been my unit number since 1982 ) I always thought about bringing the UDX name back to the airwaves. I moved back to Salt Lake City in 2019 and I thought this may be the time to get the old club going again. 

However this time around I wanted to expand outside the confines of the Zion curtain and sign up members throughout the United States. In doing that I wanted to change the name ( thanks Pam ) to something more fitted for World Wide membership, so we changed the Name to United Delta X-Ray. UDX Club now has roughly 125 Members throughout the USA and abroad, 

So proud of our community as we have some great members that promote good operations without any  H&D ( hate & discontent ) 


Way to go Men! 

Like I have been saying for years.... We want to 

MAKE CB RADIO GREAT AGAIN

Club Dues and Fees

UDX CB Radio Club does not charge Money for club membership, we rely solely on donations. We do however have club fundraisers from time to time, they are mostly done VIA You Tube live chat or you can send check/MO to our Po-Box in Salt Lake City. 

Members have been asking if they can donate equipment for auctions/ raffles. 

I like this idea but contact me before you send anything as some gear is heavy and may not be worth sending from party to party.
